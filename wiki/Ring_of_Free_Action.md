# 自由动作戒指

自由动作戒指是一件稀有[戒指](Rings.md "Rings")，它能让穿戴者无视[劣势地形](Difficult_Terrain.md "Difficult Terrain")的效果，并获得对[麻痹](Paralysed_(Condition).md "Paralysed (Condition)")和[束缚](Restrained_(Condition).md "Restrained (Condition)")状态的免疫。

宝石的核心仿佛液体般流动——但它既不含水银也不含汞。

## 属性

- [戒指](Rings.md "Rings")
- 稀有度：稀有
- 重量：0.05 千克 (0.1 磅)
- 价格：160 gp
- UID `MAG_PHB_OfFreeAction_Ring` UUID `50379f58-7892-44cb-be6e-146a60070055` Stats `MAG_PHB_OfFreeAction_Ring` ### 特殊

穿戴此物品者获得：

[坚定不移](Unwavering.md "Unwavering")
你无视[劣势地形](Difficult_Terrain_(Condition).md "Difficult Terrain (Condition)")的效果，并且不会被[麻痹](Paralysed_(Condition).md "Paralysed (Condition)")或[束缚](Restrained_(Condition).md "Restrained (Condition)")。

## 获取地点

- [月出之塔](Moonrise_Towers.md "Moonrise Towers") X: -128 Y: -193：由主层的[阿拉吉·欧布罗扎](Araj_Oblodra.md "Araj Oblodra")出售
- [下城区](Lower_City.md "Lower City") X: -92 Y: -78：如果在第二幕未从她那里获得，则由[腥红跳棋](Crimson_Draughts.md "Crimson Draughts")中的阿拉吉·欧布罗扎出售

## 备注

_关于坚定不移：_

此被动特性阻止以下状态生效：

- [麻痹](Paralyzed_(status_group).md) 状态组中的所有状态
- [束缚](Restrained_(status_group).md) 状态组中的所有状态
- [劣势地形](Difficult_Terrain_(Condition).md "Difficult Terrain (Condition)")
- 来自[熔岩](Lava.md "Lava")地表的[劣势地形：熔岩](Difficult_Terrain_colon__Lava_(Condition).md "Difficult Terrain: Lava (Condition)")
- 来自[泥巴](Mud.md "Mud")地表的[劣势地形：泥巴](Difficult_Terrain_colon__Mud_(Condition).md "Difficult Terrain: Mud (Condition)")
- 来自[扭曲的藤蔓](Twisting_Vines.md "Twisting Vines")地表的[劣势地形：藤蔓](Difficult_Terrain_colon__Vines_(Condition).md "Difficult Terrain: Vines (Condition)")
- 来自[蛛网术](Web_(surface).md "Web (surface)")地表的[劣势地形：蛛网](Difficult_Terrain_colon__Web_(Condition).md "Difficult Terrain: Web (Condition)")
- [跛足](Hamstrung_(Condition).md "Hamstrung (Condition)")
- [残废](Maimed_(Condition).md "Maimed (Condition)")
- [钉住](Pinned_Down_(Condition).md "Pinned Down (Condition)")
- 来自[植物滋长](Plant_Growth.md "Plant Growth")法术的[植物滋长](Plant_Growth_(Condition).md "Plant Growth (Condition)")

## 错误

_关于坚定不移：_

- 坚定不移无法阻止来自深水地表的[劣势地形：深水](Difficult_Terrain_colon__Deep_Water_(Condition).md "Difficult Terrain: Deep Water (Condition)")。
