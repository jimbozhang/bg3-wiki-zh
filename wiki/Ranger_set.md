# 游侠套装

The **Ranger set** includes items that interact with [Ranger](Ranger.md "Ranger") mechanics.

**游侠套装**包含与[游侠](Ranger.md "游侠")机制互动的物品。

[Hunter's Eye](Hunter's_Eye.md "Hunter's Eye")
You gain a **+1d4** bonus to [Attack rolls](Attack_roll.md "Attack roll") against creatures marked by [Hunter's Mark](Hunter's_Mark_Quarry_(Condition).md "Hunter's Mark Quarry (Condition)"), [True Strike](True_Strike_(Condition).md "True Strike (Condition)"), [Faerie Fire](Faerie_Fire_(Condition).md "Faerie Fire (Condition)"), or [Guiding Bolt](Guiding_Bolt_(Condition).md "Guiding Bolt (Condition)").

[猎人之眼](Hunter's_Eye.md "猎人之眼")
你获得 **+1d4** 奖励，对被[猎人印记](Hunter's_Mark_Quarry_(Condition).md "猎人印记猎物（状态）")、[克敌机先](True_Strike_(Condition).md "克敌机先（状态）")、[妖火](Faerie_Fire_(Condition).md "妖火（状态）")或[曳光弹](Guiding_Bolt_(Condition).md "曳光弹（状态）")标记的生物进行[攻击掷骰](Attack_roll.md "攻击掷骰")。
