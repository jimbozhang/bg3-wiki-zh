**莉尔费兹摩**是[公会](The_Guild.md "The Guild")内 Fetcher's Brats 的一员。

## Involvement

Fezmore can be found inside the [公会大厅](Guildhall.md "Guildhall"). She replaces [摩尔](Mol.md "Mol"), if Mol did not survive [第一幕](Act_One.md "Act One"), however she does not act as a trader.

## Idle banter

莉尔费兹摩 will occassionally chat with [弗洛里多](Floridor.md "Floridor"):

- Floridor
  Does it always take this long? Nothing went wrong, did it?

- Li'l Fezmor
  _Please_ , lady! Strong emotions can mess up the settling of the humours!

- Floridor
  I... I guess I don't want the humours messed up, do I?

- Floridor
  Hi, it's me again! Just wondering about that recipe.

- Li'l Fezmor
  We're working on it, all right? It's got, like, three different viscosities in it.

- Floridor
  Very well, very well. I don't want to upset the process.

- Floridor
  Is that special recipe ready yet? Just checking.

- Li'l Fezmor
  'Fraid not, saer. We're still gathering the, you know, special fluid.

- Floridor
  All right. I'll try to be patient. But he's eager.

- Floridor
  Really, I must insist on delivery of my order!

- Li'l Fezmor
  Now you've done it! The middle slime layers have inverted. I'm going to have to start over.

- Floridor
  Son of a lich! All right, start over, then.

莉尔费兹摩 will also occassionally chat with [快乐的手](Happy_Hands.md "Happy Hands"):

- Li'l Fezmor
  Happy. Another job - just a little light finger-work this time.

- Happy Hands
  I did the last one!

- Li'l Fezmor
  And lost half the coin on the cobbles. I'm giving you a chance to prove you're not a total dunce.

- Li'l Fezmor
  Happy. Thought I slipped you a job?

- Happy Hands
  Yes, boss. Just, uh... waiting for the right moment.

- Li'l Fezmor
  The right moment is when I told you to do it the first time.

- Li'l Fezmor
  Take much longer, Happy, and they'll have to call you _Smashed_ Hands instead.

- Li'l Fezmor
  Where'd Happy get to?
