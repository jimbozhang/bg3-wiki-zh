## Idle banter

莉尔费兹摩 will occassionally chat with [弗洛里多](Floridor.md "Floridor"):

- Floridor
  Does it always take this long? Nothing went wrong, did it?

- Li'l Fezmor
  _Please_ , lady! Strong emotions can mess up the settling of the humours!

- Floridor
  I... I guess I don't want the humours messed up, do I?

- Floridor
  Hi, it's me again! Just wondering about that recipe.

- Li'l Fezmor
  We're working on it, all right? It's got, like, three different viscosities in it.

- Floridor
  Very well, very well. I don't want to upset the process.

- Floridor
  Is that special recipe ready yet? Just checking.

- Li'l Fezmor
  'Fraid not, saer. We're still gathering the, you know, special fluid.

- Floridor
  All right. I'll try to be patient. But he's eager.

- Floridor
  Really, I must insist on delivery of my order!

- Li'l Fezmor
  Now you've done it! The middle slime layers have inverted. I'm going to have to start over.

- Floridor
  Son of a lich! All right, start over, then.
