## 参与情况

散塔林半精灵可以通过[召集盟友](Gather_Your_Allies_(class_action).md "Gather Your Allies (class action)")的变体[黑色焰拳执法者](Black_Fist_Enforcers.md "Black Fist Enforcers")与[散塔林魅魔](Zhentmodeus.md "Zhentmodeus")和[散塔林精灵](Zhent_Elf.md "Zhent Elf")一起被召唤，前提是队伍完成了[协助地下势力](Aid_the_Underduke.md "Aid the Underduke")并站在[散塔林会](Zhentarim.md "Zhentarim")一边。他只有在[鱼饵阿诺](Fishbait_Arno.md "Fishbait Arno")、[斯力克·伊娜塔拉](Slick_Inatra.md "Slick Inatra")、[掘墓人卡森](Gravedigger_Karcen.md "Gravedigger Karcen")和[菲斯](Phase.md "Phase")在耐色脑战斗前死亡时才会出现。
