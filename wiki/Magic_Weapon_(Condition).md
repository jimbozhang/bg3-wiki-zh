# 魔法武器 (状态)

**魔法武器**

- 武器变为魔法武器，获得+1加值至[攻击掷骰](Attack_roll.md "Attack Roll")和[伤害掷骰](Damage_Roll.md "Damage Roll")。

## 属性

[堆叠ID](Stack_ID.md "Stack ID"): `MAGIC_WEAPON` [状态组](Status_groups.md "Status groups"): [SG_RemoveOnRespec](SG_RemoveOnRespec.md "SG RemoveOnRespec")

[堆叠优先级](Stack_priority.md "Stack priority"): 1

[更多属性](Status_properties.md "Status properties"):

- [RemoveOnLongRest](Status_properties/RemoveOnLongRest.md "Status properties/RemoveOnLongRest")
