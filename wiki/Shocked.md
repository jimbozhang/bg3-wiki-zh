# 感电 (状态)

- 无法采取[反应](Reaction.md "Reaction")，且在使用[敏捷](Dexterity.md "Dexterity")的[属性检定](Ability_Check.md "Ability Check")和[豁免检定](Saving_throws.md "Saving Throws")上具有[劣势](Disadvantage.md "Disadvantage")。
