## 基本信息

- 一个来源可能因其机制产生不同效果，但本文仅列出影响近战攻击掷骰的效果。
- 如果一个来源导致影响近战攻击掷骰的状态（例如：[目盲](Blinded_(Condition).md "Blinded (Condition)")、[隐形](Invisible_(Condition).md "Invisible (Condition)")），则仅提及该状态。

## 职业特性

### [野蛮人](Barbarian.md "Barbarian")

- [野兽形态：黑猩猩](Aspect_of_the_Beast_colon__Chimpanzee.md "Aspect of the Beast: Chimpanzee") - 投掷[营地补给](Camp_supplies.md "Camp Supplies")可使目标[目盲](Blinded_(Condition).md "Blinded (Condition)")
- [野兽形态：猛虎](Aspect_of_the_Beast_colon__Tiger.md "Aspect of the Beast: Tiger") - 对[流血](Bleeding_(Condition).md "Bleeding (Condition)")或[中毒](Poisoned_(Condition).md "Poisoned (Condition)")的目标进行近战攻击掷骰时，额外增加[力量](Strength.md "Strength")调整值
- [强化魔法：恩赐](Bolstering_Magic_colon__Boon.md "Bolstering Magic: Boon") - 为自身或盟友的近战攻击掷骰添加 +1d4 奖励，持续 10 回合
- [狂暴：飞鹰之心](Rage_colon__Eagle_Heart.md "Rage: Eagle Heart") - 敌人在进行[借机攻击](Opportunity_Attacks.md "Opportunity Attacks")时具有[劣势](Disadvantage.md "Disadvantage")
- [狂暴：狂狼之心](Rage_colon__Wolf_Heart.md "Rage: Wolf Heart") - 2 米（7 英尺）内的盟友进行近战攻击掷骰时具有[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")
- [鲁莽攻击](Reckless_Attack.md "Reckless Attack") - 直到下回合前，进行近战攻击掷骰时获得[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")
- [狂野魔法：光箭](Wild_Magic_colon__Bolt_of_Light.md "Wild Magic: Bolt of Light") - 造成 1d6⁠⁠[光耀](Radiant.md "Radiant")伤害，并可能使目标[目盲](Blinded_(Condition).md "Blinded (Condition)")一回合

### [吟游诗人](Bard.md "Bard")

- [诗人激励](Bardic_Inspiration_(class_action).md "Bardic Inspiration (class action)") ([吟游诗人攻击](Bardic_Attack.md "Bardic Attack")) - 为盟友的近战攻击掷骰添加 +1d6 奖励
  - [战斗激励](Combat_Inspiration.md "Combat Inspiration") 和 [英勇攻击](Valiant_Attack.md "Valiant Attack") 对近战攻击掷骰的效果相同
- [精通诗人激励](Improved_Bardic_Inspiration_(passive_feature).md "Improved Bardic Inspiration (passive feature)") 在 5 级时将骰子提升至 +1d8，在 10 级时提升至 +1d10。
- [语出惊人](Cutting_Words.md "Cutting Words") - 对攻击生物的近战攻击掷骰施加等于你的[诗人激励](Bardic_Inspiration_(class_action).md "Bardic Inspiration (class action)")骰子的惩罚

### [牧师](Cleric.md "Cleric")

- [幽影斗篷](Cloak_of_Shadows.md "Cloak of Shadows") - 如果处于[遮蔽](Obscured.md "Obscured")状态，则变为[隐形](Cloak_of_Shadows_(Condition).md "Cloak of Shadows (Condition)")
- [导引打击](Guided_Strike.md "Guided Strike") - 近战攻击掷骰获得 +10 奖励
- [召唤分身](Invoke_Duplicity.md "Invoke Duplicity") - 对召唤幻象 3 米（10 英尺）内的生物进行近战攻击掷骰时具有[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")
  - [战神赐福](War_God's_Blessing.md "War God's Blessing") - 为 10 米（33 英尺）内盟友的近战攻击掷骰添加 +10 奖励
- [守御闪光](Warding_Flare.md "Warding Flare") - 对任何攻击生物施加[劣势](Disadvantage.md "Disadvantage")
  - [精通守御闪光](Improved_Warding_Flare.md "Improved Warding Flare") - 对攻击盟友的任何生物施加[劣势](Disadvantage.md "Disadvantage")

### [德鲁伊](Druid.md "Druid")

- [掘穴](Burrow_(Badger).md "Burrow (Badger)") - 钻入地下并在目标位置出现，可能将附近生物击倒至[倒伏](Prone_(Condition).md "Prone (Condition)")状态
- [挑衅咆哮](Goading_Roar.md "Goading Roar") - 对附近生物咆哮，可能使其[被挑衅](Goaded_(Condition).md "Goaded (Condition)")
- [雷地拳](Grounded_Thunder_Strike.md "Grounded Thunder Strike") - 用岩石和雷霆之拳猛击敌人，可能将其击倒至[倒伏](Prone_(Condition).md "Prone (Condition)")状态
- [隐形](Invisibility_(Air_Myrmidon).md "Invisibility (Air Myrmidon)") - 获得[隐形](Invisible_(Condition).md "Invisible (Condition)")
- [集群战术](Pack_Tactics.md "Pack Tactics") - 如果 3 米（10 英尺）内有盟友，则进行近战攻击掷骰时具有[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")
- [啄咬眼珠](Rend_Vision.md "Rend Vision") - 攻击生物的眼睛并使其[目盲](Blinded_(Condition).md "Blinded (Condition)")
- [猛毒之噬](Venomous_Bite_(Spider_Wild_Shape).md "Venomous Bite (Spider Wild Shape)") - 啃咬目标，可能使其[中毒](Poisoned_(Condition).md "Poisoned (Condition)")
- [蛛网](Web_(Spider_Wild_Shape).md "Web (Spider Wild Shape)") - 喷吐蛛网，可能使生物[网缚](Enwebbed_(Condition).md "Enwebbed (Condition)")

### [战士](Fighter.md "Fighter")

- [分神攻击（近战）](Distracting_Strike_(Melee).md "Distracting Strike (Melee)") - 攻击生物，可能使其[分心](Distracted_(Condition).md "Distracted (Condition)")
- [灵巧步法](Evasive_Footwork.md "Evasive Footwork") - 对自身的近战攻击掷骰施加[劣势](Disadvantage.md "Disadvantage")
- [诡诈攻击](Feinting_Attack.md "Feinting Attack") - 近战攻击掷骰获得[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")
- [挑衅攻击（近战）](Goading_Attack_(Melee).md "Goading Attack (Melee)") - 攻击生物，可能使其[被挑衅](Goaded_(Condition).md "Goaded (Condition)")
- [恐吓攻击（近战）](Menacing_Attack_(Melee).md "Menacing Attack (Melee)") - 攻击生物，可能使其[恐慌](Frightened_(Condition).md "Frightened (Condition)")
- [精准攻击](Precision_Attack.md "Precision Attack") - 为下一次近战攻击掷骰添加等于[卓越骰子](Superiority_Die.md "Superiority Die")的奖励
- [守护](Protection.md "Protection") - 携带[盾牌](Shields.md "Shields")时，对 1.5 米（5 英尺）内攻击你盟友的生物施加[劣势](Disadvantage.md "Disadvantage")
- [摔绊攻击（近战）](Trip_Attack_(Melee).md "Trip Attack (Melee)") - 攻击生物，可能将其击倒至[倒伏](Prone_(Condition).md "Prone (Condition)")状态

### [武僧](Monk.md "Monk")

- [北风之握](Clench_of_the_North_Wind.md "Clench of the North Wind") - 固定类人敌人，对其的近战攻击掷骰具有[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")
- [幽影斗篷](Cloak_of_Shadows.md "Cloak of Shadows") - 如果处于[遮蔽](Obscured.md "Obscured")状态，则变为[隐形](Cloak_of_Shadows_(Condition).md "Cloak of Shadows (Condition)")
- [凝气刚拳](Fist_of_Unbroken_Air.md "Fist of Unbroken Air") - 将生物推回，可能将其击倒至[倒伏](Prone_(Condition).md "Prone (Condition)")状态
- [疾风连击：摔绊](Flurry_of_Blows_colon__Topple.md "Flurry of Blows: Topple") - 连续出拳两次，可能将生物击倒至[倒伏](Prone_(Condition).md "Prone (Condition)")状态
- [武术：灵巧攻击](Martial_Arts_colon__Dextrous_Attacks.md "武艺：灵巧攻击") - 使用武僧武器和徒手攻击进行近战攻击掷骰时，如果敏捷更高，则使用[敏捷](Dexterity.md "Dexterity")而非[力量](Strength.md "Strength")
- [坚强防御](Patient_Defence.md "Patient Defence") - 对自身的近战攻击掷骰具有[劣势](Disadvantage.md "Disadvantage")
- [疾风之灵冲击](Rush_of_the_Gale_Spirits.md "Rush of the Gale Spirits") - 召唤强风将生物推回，迫使其[失衡](Off_Balance_(Condition).md "Off Balance (Condition)")
- [暗影技艺：躲藏](Shadow_Arts_colon__Hide.md "Shadow Arts: Hide") - 使用附赠动作[躲藏](Hiding_(Condition).md "Hiding (Condition)")，近战攻击掷骰获得[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")
- [暗影步](Shadow_Step.md "Shadow Step") - 从一个[遮蔽](Obscured.md "Obscured")区域传送至另一个，下一次近战攻击掷骰获得[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")
- [清流鞭](Water_Whip.md "Water Whip") - 可能将目标击倒至[倒伏](Prone_(Condition).md "Prone (Condition)")状态

### [圣武士](Paladin.md "Paladin")

- [破胆斥喝](Abjure_Enemy.md "Abjure Enemy") - 可能[恐慌](Frightened_(Condition).md "Frightened (Condition)")敌人
- [神圣感知](Divine_Sense.md "Divine Sense") - 对天界生物、邪魔和不死生物进行近战攻击掷骰时具有[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")
- [自然之怒](Nature's_Wrath.md "Nature's Wrath") - 可能[束缚](Nature's_Wrath_(Condition).md "Nature's Wrath (Condition)")敌人
- [守护](Protection.md "Protection") - 携带[盾牌](Shields.md "Shields")时，对 1.5 米（5 英尺）内攻击你盟友的生物施加[劣势](Disadvantage.md "Disadvantage")
- [神圣武器](Sacred_Weapon.md "Sacred Weapon") - 为近战攻击掷骰添加[魅力](Charisma.md "Charisma")调整值
- [仇敌誓言](Vow_of_Enmity.md "Vow of Enmity") - 对一名敌人的近战攻击掷骰获得[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")
  - 对自身施放[仇敌誓言](Vow_of_Enmity.md "Vow of Enmity")会使对所有生物的近战攻击掷骰获得[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")

### [游侠](Ranger.md "Ranger")

- [不祥之兆](Bad_Omen.md "Bad Omen") - 向目标投掷一根羽毛，将其标记为[被诅咒](Curse_of_the_Dire_Raven_(Condition).md "Curse of the Dire Raven (Condition)")
- [野猪冲撞](Boar_Charge.md "Boar Charge") - 向前冲锋，攻击所有生物，可能将其击倒至[倒伏](Prone_(Condition).md "Prone (Condition)")状态
- [伙伴羁绊](Companion's_Bond.md "Companion's Bond") - 召唤的伙伴获得等于你的[熟练项加值](Proficiency_Bonus.md "Proficiency bonus")的近战攻击掷骰奖励
- [惧怖伏兵：躲藏](Dread_Ambusher_colon__Hide.md "Dread Ambusher: Hide") - 使用附赠动作[躲藏](Hiding_(Condition).md "Hiding (Condition)")，近战攻击掷骰获得[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")
- [冲出重围](Escape_the_Horde.md "Escape the Horde") - 敌人的[借机攻击](Opportunity_Attacks.md "Opportunity Attacks")具有[劣势](Disadvantage.md "Disadvantage")
- [飞行](Fly_(Dire_Raven_Companion).md "Fly (Dire Raven Companion)") - 飞向目标位置，着陆时制造一团[黑暗术](Darkness.md "Darkness")
- [挑衅咆哮](Goading_Roar.md "Goading Roar") - 对附近生物咆哮，可能使其[被挑衅](Goaded_(Condition).md "Goaded (Condition)")
- [环境融合](Hide_in_Plain_Sight.md "Hide in Plain Sight") - 变为[隐形](Invisible_(Condition).md "Invisible (Condition)")
- [沾蜜利爪](Honeyed_Paws.md "Honeyed Paws") - 猛击敌人，如果敌人未持武器，可能将其击倒至[倒伏](Prone_(Condition).md "Prone (Condition)")状态
- [飞扑啃咬](Lunging_Bite.md "Lunging Bite") - 扑向目标，可能将其击倒至[倒伏](Prone_(Condition).md "Prone (Condition)")状态
- [多重攻击防御](Multiattack_Defence.md "Multiattack Defence") - 敌人攻击你时，其额外的近战攻击掷骰承受 -4 惩罚
- [集群战术](Pack_Tactics.md "Pack Tactics") - 如果 3 米（10 英尺）内有盟友，则进行近战攻击掷骰时具有[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")
- [啄咬眼珠](Rend_Vision.md "Rend Vision") - 攻击生物的眼睛并使其[目盲](Blinded_(Condition).md "Blinded (Condition)")
- [暗影幔](Umbral_Shroud.md "Umbral Shroud") - 如果处于[遮蔽](Obscured.md "Obscured")状态，则变为[隐形](Invisible_(Condition).md "Invisible (Condition)") 10 回合

### [游荡者](Rogue.md "Rogue")

- [刺杀：先攻](Assassinate_colon__Initiative.md "Assassinate: Initiative") - 对尚未行动的敌人进行近战攻击掷骰时具有[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")
- [灵巧动作：躲藏](Cunning_Action_colon__Hide.md "Cunning Action: Hide") - 使用附赠动作[躲藏](Hiding_(Condition).md "Hiding (Condition)")，近战攻击掷骰获得[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")
- [诈术法师之手](Mage_Hand_Legerdemain.md "Mage Hand Legerdemain") - [法师之手](Mage_Hand.md "Mage Hand")变为[隐形](Invisible_(Condition).md "Invisible (Condition)")
- [极效潜行](Supreme_Sneak.md "Supreme Sneak") - 完全融入环境，变为[隐形](Invisible_(Condition).md "Invisible (Condition)")

### [术士](Sorcerer.md "Sorcerer")

- [扭曲幸运](Bend_Luck.md "Bend Luck") - 消耗 2 术法点，对目标的近战攻击掷骰施加 +1d4 或 -1d4
- [混沌之潮](Tides_of_Chaos.md "Tides of Chaos") - 下一次近战攻击掷骰获得[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")
- [狂野魔法：朦胧术](Wild_Magic_colon__Blur.md "Wild Magic: Blur") - 9 米（30 英尺）内的所有生物处于[模糊](Blurred_(Condition).md "Blurred (Condition)")状态
- [狂野魔法：纠缠术](Wild_Magic_colon__Entangle.md "Wild Magic: Entangle") - 在 4 米（13 英尺）内制造藤蔓地表，可能使所有触及的生物[缠绕](Entangled_(Condition).md "Entangled (Condition)")
- [狂野魔法：浓雾](Wild_Magic_colon__Fog.md "Wild Magic: Fog") - 制造一团浓雾，使范围内的所有生物[目盲](Blinded_(Condition).md "Blinded (Condition)")

### [邪术师](Warlock.md "Warlock")

- [熵光结界](Entropic_Ward.md "Entropic Ward") - 对自身的近战攻击掷骰施加[劣势](Disadvantage.md "Disadvantage")，如果攻击未命中，则对攻击者进行近战攻击掷骰时获得[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")
- [妖精仪态：扰人](Fey_Presence_colon__Disturbing.md "Fey Presence: Disturbing") - 可能[恐慌](Frightened_(Condition).md "Frightened (Condition)")附近敌人
- [雾遁](Misty_Escape.md "Misty Escape") - 受到伤害时变为[隐形](Invisible_(Condition).md "Invisible (Condition)")
- [催命警示](Mortal_Reminder.md "Mortal Reminder") - 造成[重击](Critical_Hit.md "Critical Hit")时，可能[恐慌](Frightened_(Condition).md "Frightened (Condition)")所有附近敌人
- [刃之魔契](Pact_of_the_Blade_(passive_feature).md "Pact of the Blade (passive feature)") - 使用魔契武器进行近战攻击掷骰时，使用[魅力](Charisma.md "Charisma")调整值而非[力量](Strength.md "Strength")或[敏捷](Dexterity.md "Dexterity")

### [法师](Wizard.md "Wizard")

- [预兆](Portent.md "Portent") - 使用反应将 18 米（60 英尺）内任何近战攻击掷骰的骰子更改为你的预兆骰之一
