# 伏击 (状态)

**伏击**

- 受影响实体将在敌人进入攻击范围时立即发起攻击。只有通过成功的[察觉技能](Perception.md "Perception") [检定](Check.md "Check")才能发现它。

## 属性

[堆叠ID](Stack_ID.md "Stack ID"): `AMBUSH` [堆叠优先级](Stack_priority.md "Stack priority"): 0

[更多属性](Status_properties.md "Status properties"):

- [禁用头顶显示](Status_properties/DisableOverhead.md "Status properties/DisableOverhead")
- [禁用战斗日志](Status_properties/DisableCombatlog.md "Status properties/DisableCombatlog")

## 备注

- 受影响实体不会对[初级幻影](Minor_Illusion.md "Minor Illusion")做出反应。
