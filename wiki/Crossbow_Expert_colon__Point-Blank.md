# 强弩专家：近距平射

**强弩专家：近距平射** 是一项被动功能，允许你在近战范围内进行弩攻击，而不会获得[劣势](Disadvantage.md "Disadvantage")。

## 描述

当你在近战范围内进行弩攻击时，[攻击掷骰](Attack_rolls.md "Attack Rolls")不会具有[劣势](Disadvantage.md "Disadvantage")。

## 如何习得

由专长授予：[强弩专家](Crossbow_Expert.md "Crossbow Expert")

被以下生物使用：

- [Add'ath](Add'ath.md "Add'ath"), [Aggralix](Aggralix.md "Aggralix"), [Ahu'u'rag](Ahu'u'rag.md "Ahu'u'rag"), [Arrinye](Arrinye.md "Arrinye"), [Bella (Wyrm's Rock Fortress)](Bella_(Wyrm's_Rock_Fortress).md), [Chost](Chost.md "Chost"), [Chy'raagh](Chy'raagh.md "Chy'raagh"), [Clover](Clover.md "Clover"), [Dart Dirtwood](Dart_Dirtwood.md "Dart Dirtwood"), [Debin](Debin.md "Debin"), [Enver Gortash](Enver_Gortash.md "Enver Gortash"), [Equidna](Equidna.md "Equidna"), [G'r'ath](G'r'ath.md "G'r'ath"), [Gai](Gai.md "Gai"), [Glenvora](Glenvora.md "Glenvora"), [Gneiss](Gneiss.md "Gneiss"), [Gonfalon](Gonfalon.md "Gonfalon"), [Griff](Griff.md "Griff"), [Gummidge](Gummidge.md "Gummidge"), [Hagedorrn](Hagedorrn.md "Hagedorrn"), [Havard](Havard.md "Havard"), [Helag](Helag.md "Helag"), [Jy'ir](Jy'ir.md "Jy'ir"), [Kethk](Kethk.md "Kethk"), [Kharyk](Kharyk.md "Kharyk"), [Ko'kuu](Ko'kuu.md "Ko'kuu"), [Kyberos](Kyberos.md "Kyberos"), [Kyrr'a'ath](Kyrr'a'ath.md "Kyrr'a'ath"), [Larche](Larche.md "Larche"), [Lionel (Rivington)](Lionel_(Rivington).md), [Lord Shattershield's Attendant](Lord_Shattershield's_Attendant.md "Lord Shattershield's Attendant"), [Loudstone](Loudstone.md "Loudstone"), [Ly'iss](Ly'iss.md "Ly'iss"), [Madigan](Madigan.md "Madigan"), [Magnolia](Magnolia.md "Magnolia"), [Mibbs](Mibbs.md "Mibbs"), [Mîran](M%C3%AEran.md "Mîran"), [Nimba'as](Nimba'as.md "Nimba'as"), [Nobster](Nobster.md "Nobster"), [Olloa](Olloa.md "Olloa"), [Pard](Pard.md "Pard"), [Pol](Pol.md "Pol"), [Ra'gur](Ra'gur.md "Ra'gur"), [Rockbothem](Rockbothem.md "Rockbothem"), [Seyama](Seyama.md "Seyama"), [Un'n](Un'n.md "Un'n"), [Voss' Raider](Voss'_Raider.md "Voss' Raider"), [Wearne](Wearne.md "Wearne"), [Zar'an](Zar'an.md "Zar'an"), [Zastri](Zastri.md "Zastri"), [Zeddi'in](Zeddi'in.md "Zeddi'in"), [Zerentha](Zerentha.md "Zerentha"), [Zunii](Zunii.md "Zunii")

## 备注

- 由于[受威胁](Threatened_(Condition).md "Threatened (Condition)")的机制，当近战范围内有两个或更多敌人时，此专长将无效。仅当近战范围内只有一个敌人且攻击针对该敌人时，此专长才有帮助。

## 简要预览

近战范围内的弩攻击不会获得[劣势](Disadvantage.md "Disadvantage")。
