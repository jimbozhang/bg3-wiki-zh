A **金戒指**是[戒指](Rings.md "戒指")的一种常见类型。主要用于与[商人](Traders.md "商人")[议价](Barter.md "议价")或出售。

这枚戒指的内侧曾刻有一个日期，但大部分早已被时间磨损殆尽。

## 属性

- [戒指](Rings.md "戒指")

- 稀有度：普通

- 重量：0.05 kg (0.1 lb)

- 价格：20 gp

- UID `LOOT_GEN_Ring_A_Gem_A_Gold` UUID `173aad0e-0db4-4f2f-8f24-49e6898b8f90` ## 获取地点

- 在游戏中的宝箱里，以及角色携带

# 变体

A **Gold Ring** is a common type of [Ring](Rings.md "Rings"). Primarily of use for barter or sale to [Traders](Traders.md "Traders").

A date was once engraved on the inside of this ring, but most of it has long since been worn away by time.
