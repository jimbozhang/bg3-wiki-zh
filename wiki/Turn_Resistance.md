# 驱散抗性

**驱散抗性**在对抗任何驱散不死生物的效果时，给予[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")进行[豁免检定](Saving_throws.md "Saving Throws")。

## 描述

**驱散抗性**在对抗任何驱散不死生物的效果时，给予[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")进行[豁免检定](Saving_throw.md "Saving Throw")。

## 如何习得

由以下生物使用：

- [安苏](Ansur.md "Ansur")、[巴萨扎尔](Balthazar.md "Balthazar")、[阿巴济戈的回声](Echo_of_Abazigal.md "Echo of Abazigal")、[阿米利桑的回声](Echo_of_Amelyssan.md "Echo of Amelyssan")、[伊拉塞拉的回声](Echo_of_Illasera.md "Echo of Illasera")、[森代的回声](Echo_of_Sendai.md "Echo of Sendai")、[格灵哥斯·索姆](Gerringothe_Thorm.md "Gerringothe Thorm")、[吉斯列夫](Ghislev.md "Ghislev")、[大僵尸](Greater_Zombie.md "Greater Zombie")、[马鲁斯·索姆](Malus_Thorm.md "Malus Thorm")、[秘术师卡里翁](Mystic_Carrion.md "Mystic Carrion")、[弃誓骑士](Oathbreaker_Knight.md "Oathbreaker Knight")、[西索博得·索姆](Thisobald_Thorm.md "Thisobald Thorm")、[斯郎博](Thrumbo.md "Thrumbo")、[懦弱面容](Visage_of_Cowardice.md "Visage of Cowardice")、[贪婪面容](Visage_of_Greed.md "Visage of Greed")、[负罪面容](Visage_of_Guilt.md "Visage of Guilt")、[冷漠面容](Visage_of_Heartlessness.md "Visage of Heartlessness")、[服从面容](Visage_of_Obedience.md "Visage of Obedience")、[悔恨面容](Visage_of_Regret.md "Visage of Regret")
