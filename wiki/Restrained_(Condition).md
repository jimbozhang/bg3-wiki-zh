# 束缚 (状态)

**束缚**

- 无法移动。针对受影响实体的[攻击掷骰](Attack_roll.md "Attack Roll")具有[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")，而该实体的[攻击掷骰](Attack_roll.md "Attack Roll")和[敏捷](Dexterity.md "Dexterity")[豁免检定](Saving_throw.md "Saving Throw")具有[劣势](Disadvantage.md "Disadvantage")

## 属性

[堆叠ID](Stack_ID.md "Stack ID"): `RESTRAINED` [状态组](Status_groups.md "Status groups"): [SG_Restrained](SG_Restrained.md "SG Restrained"), [SG_Condition](SG_Condition.md "SG Condition")
