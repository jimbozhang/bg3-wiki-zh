# 伏击 (状态)

**伏击**

- 对受影响实体的[攻击掷骰](Attack_roll.md "Attack Roll")具有[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")，且其在[力量](Strength.md "Strength")和[敏捷](Dexterity.md "Dexterity")[检定](Checks.md "Checks")上具有[劣势](Disadvantage.md "Disadvantage")。

- 受到伤害或被[协助](Help.md "Help")会结束此状态。

## 属性

[堆叠 ID](Stack_ID.md "Stack ID"): `DOPPELGANGER_OFFBALANCE` [状态组](Status_groups.md "Status groups"): [SG_Helpable_Condition](SG_Helpable_Condition.md "SG Helpable Condition")

[持续时间结束](Conditions.md#Duration "Conditions"): 驱散时

[堆叠优先级](Stack_priority.md "Stack priority"): 5

[更多属性](Status_properties.md "Status properties"):

- [InitiateCombat](Status_properties/InitiateCombat.md "Status properties/InitiateCombat")
