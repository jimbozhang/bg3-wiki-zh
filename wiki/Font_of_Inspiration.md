# 激励之泉

**激励之泉**是[Bard](Bard.md "Bard")的被动特性，它使你在长休或短休后恢复所有[Bardic Inspiration](Bardic_Inspiration_(class_action).md "Bardic Inspiration (class action)") 冲锋。

## 描述

你在一次[Long](Long_Rest.md "Long Rest")或[Short Rest](Short_rest.md "Short Rest")后恢复所有你的[Bardic Inspirations](Bardic_Inspiration_(resource).md)。

## 如何习得

职业：

- 职业等级 5: [Bard](Bard.md "Bard")

生物使用：

- [Amira](Amira.md "Amira"), [Dimples Backster](Dimples_Backster.md "Dimples Backster"), [Hiero Lue](Hiero_Lue.md "Hiero Lue"), [Lady Croup](Lady_Croup.md "Lady Croup"), [Lorlarrio](Lorlarrio.md "Lorlarrio"), [Mask of Servitude (The Blushing Mermaid)](Mask_of_Servitude_(The_Blushing_Mermaid).md), [Milil](Milil.md "Milil"), [Oskar Fevras](Oskar_Fevras.md "Oskar Fevras"), [Roah Moonglow](Roah_Moonglow.md "Roah Moonglow"), [The Snapping of Strings](The_Snapping_of_Strings.md "The Snapping of Strings"), [Viola Silvertongue](Viola_Silvertongue.md "Viola Silvertongue")

## 简要预览

在一次长休或短休后恢复所有[Bardic Inspiration](Bardic_Inspiration_(resource).md)。
