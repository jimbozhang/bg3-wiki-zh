# 反魅惑光环 (状态)

**反魅惑光环**

- 你和9米（30英尺）范围内的盟友在对抗被[魅惑](Charmed_(status_group).md)和[恐慌](Frightened_(status_group).md)的[豁免检定](Saving_throw.md "Saving Throw")上具有[优势](Advantage.md "Advantage")。

## 属性

[堆叠ID](Stack_ID.md "Stack ID"): `COUNTERCHARM_AURA` [状态组](Status_groups.md "Status groups"): [SG_RemoveOnRespec](SG_RemoveOnRespec.md "SG RemoveOnRespec")

**[反魅惑](Countercharm_(Condition).md "反魅惑 (状态)")**
