## 文本

灾祸术机密。虽然与我们崇拜 ⁠[米尔寇](Myrkul.md "米尔寇") 和 ⁠[巴尔](Bhaal.md "巴尔") 的兄弟姐妹合作很重要，但要记住 ⁠[灾祸术](Bane_(God).md) is the Tyrant, it is Bane who rules. Subversion and capture of [Gondian](Gond.md "Gond") operations in Baldur's Gate is a Banite project, particularly as regards the [gnomish](Gondian.md "Gondian") artificers. They are ours - or, more particularly, MINE. They are not to be tadpoled, as that would expose them to control through the [Absolute](Absolute.md "Absolute") hierarchy. Instead, they are to be locked in Incineration [Collars](Kill_Collar.md "Kill Collar") that are remote controlled by [Prinski's](Waldemar_Prinski.md "瓦尔德马·普林斯基") [Motivators](Motivator.md "激发器"). I want those gnomes aware of what they are doing and deeply unhappy about it. When the [Watcher Project](Steel_Watch_Foundry.md "钢铁卫士铸造厂") is complete and the artificers are of no further use, the survivors will then be tadpoled.

\- Lord Gortash
