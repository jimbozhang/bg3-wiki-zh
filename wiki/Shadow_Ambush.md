# 阴影伏击

**阴影伏击**是一种被动特性，允许此生物在从阴影中攻击时造成 4d8⁠⁠[黯蚀](Necrotic.md "黯蚀") 伤害。

## 描述

此生物在从阴影中攻击时造成额外的 4d8⁠⁠[黯蚀](Necrotic.md "黯蚀") 伤害。

## 如何习得

由以下生物使用：

- [Afara](Afara.md "Afara"), [Baxta](Baxta.md "Baxta"), [Bluenail](Bluenail.md "Bluenail"), [Dohna](Dohna.md "Dohna"), [Dollay](Dollay.md "Dollay"), [Fredrichk](Fredrichk.md "Fredrichk"), [Garrigan](Garrigan.md "Garrigan"), [Gloommask](Gloommask.md "Gloommask"), [Greymask](Greymask.md "Greymask"), [Gydd](Gydd.md "Gydd"), [Hendly](Hendly.md "Hendly"), [Humboldt](Humboldt.md "Humboldt"), [Hyrald](Hyrald.md "Hyrald"), [Imwise](Imwise.md "Imwise"), [Lamona](Lamona.md "Lamona"), [Monteith](Monteith.md "Monteith"), [Morann](Morann.md "Morann"), [Nightmist](Nightmist.md "Nightmist"), [Owltalon](Owltalon.md "Owltalon"), [莎尔学徒](Sharran_Novice.md "Sharran Novice"), [Sharran Sentry](Sharran_Sentry.md "Sharran Sentry"), [Tylda](Tylda.md "Tylda"), [Uttermask](Uttermask.md "Uttermask"), [Wilbur](Wilbur.md "Wilbur")
