# 微光戒指

微光戒指是一个[戒指](Rings.md "Rings")，品质为精良，可使[魅力](Charisma.md "Charisma")[豁免检定](Saving_throw.md "Saving Throw")+1，并使[感知](Wisdom.md "Wisdom")[豁免检定](Saving_throw.md "Saving Throw")-1。

这枚戒指中央的石榴石宛如一只不眨眼的、充满恶意的[眼睛](Eye.md "Eye")。

## 属性

- [戒指](Rings.md "Rings")
- 品质：精良
- 重量：0.05 千克 (0.1 磅)
- 价格：45 金币
- UID `MAG_TheOptimist_Ring` UUID `743984c8-55a8-4045-9904-f0c84a0cf692` ### 特殊

佩戴此物品者获得：

- [魅力](Charisma.md "Charisma") [豁免检定](Saving_throw.md "Saving Throw") +1
- [感知](Wisdom.md "Wisdom") [豁免检定](Saving_throw.md "Saving Throw") -1

## 获取地点

- [卡姆的服装店](Carm's_Garms.md "Carm's Garms") X: 23 Y: 131：在[飞龙关](Wyrm's_Crossing.md "Wyrm's Crossing")与[纳贝尔](Naaber.md "Naaber")多次对话直至所有[对话](Dialogue.md "Dialogue")选项[力竭](Exhausted.md "Exhausted")后，作为奖励获得

## 备注

- 只有当先从他身上[扒窃](Pickpocket.md "Pickpocket")了[绝对信心护符](Absolute_Confidence_Amulet.md "Absolute Confidence Amulet")后，才会将此戒指交给队伍。
