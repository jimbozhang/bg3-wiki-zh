# Shattered Flail

Shattered Flail is a rare +2 [Mace](Maces.md "Maces") that heals the wielder when they hit an enemy - at the cost of potentially going [mad](Madness_(Condition).md "Madness (Condition)") when they stop attacking.

损坏的链枷是一把稀有的+2[硬头锤](Maces.md "硬头锤")，当持有者击中敌人时会治疗自己——但代价是如果停止攻击，可能会陷入[疯狂](Madness_(Condition).md "疯狂（状态）")。

A bundle of bones and blood - and a soul-piercing demonic presence - is all that remains of the Flind's flail. It would make a decent mace with a particularly nasty bite.

一堆骨头和血液——以及一种刺穿灵魂的恶魔存在——就是弗林德的链枷所剩下的全部。它会成为一把不错的硬头锤，带有特别恶毒的啃咬。

## Notes

_Regarding Yeenoghu's Gift:_

- After hitting a creature with the Shattered Flail, the wielder gains **Yeenoghu's Gift** for 6 turns. For the duration, they must succeed a [DC](Dice_rolls.md#Save_DCs "Dice rolls") 10 [Wisdom](Wisdom.md "Wisdom") [saving throw](Saving_throw.md "Saving throw") at the end of any turn on which they do not hit an enemy, or go **Mad** for 1 turn.
- The [Saving Throw](Saving_throw.md "Saving Throw") rolls made by the wielder of this weapon do not appear in the combat log, but they are in fact being rolled at the end of each turn where the wielder _does not damage an enemy_.
- The duration of **Yeenoghu's Gift** is not refreshed by hitting enemies again; it only lasts the base 6 turns, after which it can be re-applied.
- While **Mad** , the wielder is uncontrollably hostile to all nearby creatures, including allies, and uses any and all tools at their disposal to inflict harm. This could include using powerful [Ammunition](Ammunition.md "Ammunition") or [Consumables](Consumables.md "Consumables") from their inventory, casting high level [Spells](Spell.md "Spell"), or [shoving](Shove.md "Shove") allies to their deaths.
- Although the combat log states "Tav healed self...", this healing does _not_ trigger other items which activate on the character's healing such as [Boots of Aid and Comfort](Boots_of_Aid_and_Comfort.md "Boots of Aid and Comfort"), [The Whispering Promise](The_Whispering_Promise.md "The Whispering Promise") or [Broodmother's Revenge](Broodmother's_Revenge.md "Broodmother's Revenge").
- The wielder is healed upon _each instance of damage_. This means that in the case of attacks which deal multiple damage types, such as [Booming Blade](Booming_Blade.md "Booming Blade") and [Divine Smite](Divine_Smite.md "Divine Smite") (as well as other [Smite](Smite.md "Smite") spells), or in the case of attacks which damage multiple targets, such as [Slashing Flourish (Melee)](Slashing_Flourish_(Melee).md "Slashing Flourish (Melee)"), the wielder is healed multiple times.

## 注释

_关于伊诺胡之馈赠：_

- 用损坏的链枷击中生物后，持有者获得**伊诺胡之馈赠**，持续6回合。在此期间，如果他们在任何回合结束时没有击中敌人，则必须通过一次[DC](Dice_rolls.md#Save_DCs "掷骰") 10的[感知](Wisdom.md "感知")[豁免检定](Saving_throw.md "豁免检定")，否则将陷入**疯狂**1回合。
- 此武器持有者进行的[豁免检定](Saving_throw.md "豁免检定")掷骰不会出现在战斗日志中，但实际上它们是在持有者_未对敌人造成伤害_的每个回合结束时进行的。
- **伊诺胡之馈赠**的持续时间不会因再次击中敌人而刷新；它只持续基础的6回合，之后可以重新施加。
- 当**疯狂**时，持有者会不可控地敌视所有附近的生物，包括盟友，并使用他们手头的任何工具造成伤害。这可能包括使用库存中强大的[弹药](Ammunition.md "弹药")或[消耗品](Consumables.md "消耗品")，施放高等级[法术](Spell.md "法术")，或将盟友[推击](Shove.md "推击")致死。
- 尽管战斗日志显示“塔夫治疗了自己...”，但此治疗_不会_触发其他在角色治疗时激活的物品，例如[疗愈之靴](Boots_of_Aid_and_Comfort.md "疗愈之靴")、[轻声承诺](The_Whispering_Promise.md "轻声承诺")或[巢穴之母的复仇](Broodmother's_Revenge.md "巢穴之母的复仇")。
- 持有者在_每次伤害实例_时都会被治疗。这意味着在造成多种伤害类型的攻击情况下，例如[轰鸣剑](Booming_Blade.md "轰鸣剑")和[至圣斩](Divine_Smite.md "至圣斩")（以及其他[至圣斩](Smite.md "至圣斩")法术），或者在攻击多个目标的情况下，例如[挥砍华舞（近战）](Slashing_Flourish_(Melee).md "挥砍华舞（近战）")，持有者会被多次治疗。
