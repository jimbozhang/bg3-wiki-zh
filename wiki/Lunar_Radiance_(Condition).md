# 月光光耀 (状态)

**月光光耀**

- 受影响实体的[攻击掷骰](Attack_roll.md "Attack Roll")具有-2减值。它还会在周围区域散发明亮光线。

## 属性

[堆叠ID](Stack_ID.md "Stack ID"): `MAG_RADIANT_RADIATING_ORB` [堆叠优先级](Stack_priority.md "Stack priority"): 1

[更多属性](Status_properties.md "Status properties"):

- [禁用战斗日志](Status_properties/DisableCombatlog.md "Status properties/DisableCombatlog")
