# 灼烧 (状态)

**灼烧**

- 每回合受到 1d10⁠⁠[火焰](Fire.md "Fire") 伤害。

## 属性

[堆叠 ID](Stack_ID.md "Stack ID"): `BURNING` [状态组](Status_groups.md "Status groups"): [SG_Surface](SG_Surface.md "SG Surface"), [SG_Light](SG_Light.md "SG Light")

[持续时间减少](Conditions.md#Duration "Conditions"): 回合开始时

[更多属性](Status_properties.md "Status properties"):

- [InitiateCombat](Status_properties/InitiateCombat.md "Status properties/InitiateCombat")
- [Burning](Status_properties/Burning.md "Status properties/Burning")
- [DisableOverhead](Status_properties/DisableOverhead.md "Status properties/DisableOverhead")
- [IgnoreResting](Status_properties/IgnoreResting.md "Status properties/IgnoreResting")
