## 任意地点

_这些激励点可在整个游戏过程中获得，超越第一幕。_

### 通用

⁠[命运必须等待](../Acolyte.md#Fate_Must_Wait "Acolyte")：复活队伍中的任何人。[侍僧](../Acolyte.md "Acolyte")背景。[影心](../Shadowheart.md "Shadowheart") [齐尼斯·弗'赛尔](../Zenith_Feur'sel.md "Zenith Feur'sel")
⁠[巨龙财宝](../Noble.md#Dragon's_Hoard "Noble")：队伍中至少有2000金币。[贵族](../Noble.md "Noble")背景。[明塔拉](../Minthara.md "Minthara") [克尔兹](../Kerz.md "Kerz")
⁠[坟墓的低语](../Sage.md#Whispers_from_the_Grave "Sage")：通过[与死者交谈](../The_Amulet_of_Lost_Voices.md "The Amulet of Lost Voices")获得一些智慧。[学者](../Sage.md "Sage")背景。[盖尔](../Gale.md "Gale") [福泽朗普爵士](../Sir_Fuzzalump.md "Sir_Fuzzalump")
⁠[出售灵魂](../Noble.md#Soul_for_Sale "Noble")：获得一枚[灵魂硬币](../Soul_Coin.md#Where_to_find "Soul Coin")。[贵族](../Noble.md "Noble")背景。[明塔拉](../Minthara.md "Minthara") [克尔兹](../Kerz.md "Kerz")

### 战斗

⁠[杀戮狂潮](../Haunted_One.md#Killing_Spree "Haunted One")：单回合内击杀5名敌人。[邪念缠身](../Haunted_One.md "Haunted One")背景。[邪念](../Dark_Urge.md "Dark Urge")
⁠[五杀](../Soldier.md#Pentacrush "Soldier")：单回合内击杀5名敌人。[士兵](../Soldier.md "Soldier")背景。[莱埃泽尔](../Lae'zel.md "Lae'zel") [瓦兰娜·繁日](../Varanna_Sunblossom.md "Varanna Sunblossom")
⁠[不友好火力](../Haunted_One.md#Unfriendly_Fire "Haunted One")：单回合内击败5名中立生物。[邪念缠身](../Haunted_One.md "Haunted One")背景。[邪念](../Dark_Urge.md "Dark Urge")
⁠[传世之战](../Soldier.md#One_for_the_Ages "Soldier")：在队伍击杀十名或更多敌人的战斗中存活下来。[士兵](../Soldier.md "Soldier")背景。[莱埃泽尔](../Lae'zel.md "Lae'zel") [瓦兰娜·繁日](../Varanna_Sunblossom.md "Varanna Sunblossom")
⁠[痛苦盛典](../Haunted_One.md#Panoply_of_Agony "Haunted One")：用每种[伤害类型](../Damage.md#Damage_types "Damage")至少击杀一名敌人。[邪念缠身](../Haunted_One.md "Haunted One")背景。[邪念](../Dark_Urge.md "Dark Urge")
⁠[战略家](../Soldier.md#Strategist "Soldier")：一击击败强大的敌人。[士兵](../Soldier.md "Soldier")背景。[莱埃泽尔](../Lae'zel.md "Lae'zel") [瓦兰娜·繁日](../Varanna_Sunblossom.md "Varanna Sunblossom")

### 对话

⁠[放纵汝之欲望](../Haunted_One.md#Indulge_Thine_Appetites "Haunted One")：追随邪念。[邪念缠身](../Haunted_One.md "Haunted One")背景。[邪念](../Dark_Urge.md "Dark Urge")
⁠[克制汝之欲望](../Haunted_One.md#Subdue_Thine_Appetites "Haunted One")：抵抗邪念。[邪念缠身](../Haunted_One.md "Haunted One")背景。[邪念](../Dark_Urge.md "Dark Urge")

邪念的选择没有标签，但通常是暴力的，并使用较细的字体。必须特别选择放纵或抵抗邪念的选项，而不仅仅是忽略它。
⁠[古异权威](../Noble.md#Eldritch_Authority "Noble")：成为[真魂者](../True_Soul.md "True Soul")。[贵族](../Noble.md "Noble")背景 [明塔拉](../Minthara.md "Minthara") [克尔兹](../Kerz.md "Kerz")

使用[灵吸怪](../Illithid_Persuasion.md "Illithid Persuasion")游说7次。
⁠[复生者](../Sage.md#Reanimator "Sage")：正确遵循盖尔的指示来[复活](../In_Case_of_Death....md "In Case of Death...")他。[学者](../Sage.md "Sage")背景。[盖尔](../Gale.md "Gale") [福泽朗普爵士](../Sir_Fuzzalump.md "Sir_Fuzzalump")

只有与小袋互动的角色才能获得激励点。
⁠[外交倾向](../Noble.md#Penchant_for_Diplomacy "Noble")：与一名伙伴达到[好感度](../Approval.md "Approval")高等级（45+）。[贵族](../Noble.md "Noble")背景。[明塔拉](../Minthara.md "Minthara") [克尔兹](../Kerz.md "Kerz")

### 犯罪

⁠[逃脱专家](../Criminal.md#Escape_Artist "Criminal")：被捕后从监狱逃脱。[罪犯](../Criminal.md "Criminal")背景。[维'瑞尔·文基尔](../Ver'yll_Wenkiir.md "Ver'yll Wenkiir")
⁠[血的代价](../Criminal.md#Blood_Price "Criminal")：杀害无辜平民。[罪犯](../Criminal.md "Criminal")背景。[维'瑞尔·文基尔](../Ver'yll_Wenkiir.md "Ver'yll Wenkiir")
⁠[丰厚财富](../Criminal.md#A_Fine_Fortune "Criminal")：窃取价值至少1000金币的物品。[罪犯](../Criminal.md "Criminal")背景。[维'瑞尔·文基尔](../Ver'yll_Wenkiir.md "Ver'yll Wenkiir")
⁠[我无所谓](../Noble.md#Fine_By_Me "Noble")：使用玩家角色的财富贿赂守卫。[贵族](../Noble.md "Noble")背景。[明塔拉](../Minthara.md "Minthara") [克尔兹](../Kerz.md "Kerz")

### 交易

⁠[尊贵客户](../Guild_Artisan.md#Valued_Customer "Guild Artisan")：与好感度高的人交易。[公会工匠](../Guild_Artisan.md "Guild Artisan")背景。[杰斯琳](../Jacelyn.md "Jacelyn")
⁠[黄金杰作](../Guild_Artisan.md#A_Golden_Masterpiece "Guild Artisan")：出售价值至少1000金币的物品。[公会工匠](../Guild_Artisan.md "Guild Artisan")背景。[杰斯琳](../Jacelyn.md "Jacelyn")

## 利文顿

### 阿尔弗的宅邸

**与[阿尔弗](../Arfur_Gregorio.md "Arfur Gregorio")交谈：**

⁠[供需关系](../Criminal.md#Supply_and_Demand "Criminal")：提出以低于利文顿老鼠帮的费用赶走占屋者。[罪犯](../Criminal.md "Criminal")背景。[维'瑞尔·文基尔](../Ver'yll_Wenkiir.md "Ver'yll Wenkiir")
⁠[多数人的需求](../Acolyte.md#Needs_of_the_Many "Acolyte")：说服商人让难民占屋者留在他家中。[侍僧](../Acolyte.md "Acolyte")背景。[影心](../Shadowheart.md "Shadowheart") [齐尼斯·弗'赛尔](../Zenith_Feur'sel.md "Zenith Feur'sel")

两者均可获得。队伍必须先说服阿尔弗，队伍成员可以以半价完成。然后以任何方式处理泽诺维亚，再与阿尔弗交谈并恐吓他让占屋者留下。

**保护[戈莱维茨](../Ben_Golewits.md "Ben Golewits")：**

⁠[行使审判](../Noble.md#Exerting_Judgement "Noble")：结束商人与占屋者之间的争吵。[贵族](../Noble.md "Noble")背景。[明塔拉](../Minthara.md "Minthara") [克尔兹](../Kerz.md "Kerz")

任何解决方案都会触发此激励点。
⁠[确保款待](../Folk_Hero.md#Securing_Hospitality "Folk Hero")：拯救占屋者免遭驱逐。[平民英雄](../Folk_Hero.md "Folk Hero")背景。[威尔](../Wyll.md "Wyll") [明斯克](../Minsc.md "Minsc") [丹顿](../Danton.md "Danton")
⁠[发现者保留](../Urchin.md#Finders_Keepers "Urchin")：帮助占屋者保住临时住所。[孤儿](../Urchin.md "Urchin")背景。[玛德拉·死眼](../Maddala_Deadeye.md "Maddala Deadeye")

队伍必须说服阿尔弗让占屋者留下，然后与本交谈。

### 强制征用的谷仓

_与[内斯特](../Nestor.md "Nestor")交谈：_

⁠[施舍不幸者](../Noble.md#Alms_for_the_Unfortunate "Noble")：向难民捐赠物品。[贵族](../Noble.md "Noble")背景。[明塔拉](../Minthara.md "Minthara") [克尔兹](../Kerz.md "Kerz")
⁠[分享战利品](../Soldier.md#Sharing_the_Spoils "Soldier")：向难民捐赠。[士兵](../Soldier.md "Soldier")背景。[莱埃泽尔](../Lae'zel.md "Lae'zel") [贾希拉](../Jaheira.md "Jaheira") [瓦兰娜·繁日](../Varanna_Sunblossom.md "Varanna Sunblossom")
⁠[尽力而为](../Urchin.md#Spare_What_You_Can "Urchin")：向大谷仓捐赠。[孤儿](../Urchin.md "Urchin")背景。[玛德拉·死眼](../Maddala_Deadeye.md "Maddala Deadeye")

队伍必须捐赠物品。
⁠[灵魂食粮](../Acolyte.md#Food_for_the_Soul "Acolyte")：向捐赠箱捐赠一次长休所需的食物。[侍僧](../Acolyte.md "Acolyte")背景。[影心](../Shadowheart.md "Shadowheart") [齐尼斯·弗'赛尔](../Zenith_Feur'sel.md "Zenith Feur'sel")

队伍必须捐赠一个补给包。
⁠[荒野的馈赠](../Outlander.md#Gifts_of_the_Wilds "Outlander")：捐赠原材料。[化外之民](../Outlander.md "Outlander")背景。[卡菈克](../Karlach.md "Karlach") [哈尔辛](../Halsin.md "Halsin") [埃尔德拉·卢斯琳](../Eldra_Luthrinn.md "Eldra Luthrinn") [希娜'兹斯](../Sina'zith.md "Sina'zith")

队伍必须捐赠一些食物。

**探索[谷仓](../Requisitioned_Barn.md "Requisitioned Barn")：**

⁠[勿信诚实之人](../Charlatan.md#Never_Trust_an_Honest_Person "Charlatan")：撒谎以进入大谷仓。[骗子](../Charlatan.md "Charlatan")背景。[阿斯代伦](../Astarion.md "Astarion") [克瑞·德瑞克](../Kree_Derryck.md "Kree Derryck")

队伍必须未经许可进入谷仓，然后让自己被护送出去，并对[内斯特](../Nestor.md "Nestor")成功通过欺瞒检定。
⁠[捐赠即免费](../Criminal.md#Donations_Means_'Free' "Criminal")：从大谷仓窃取捐赠物品。[罪犯](../Criminal.md "Criminal")背景。[维'瑞尔·文基尔](../Ver'yll_Wenkiir.md "Ver'yll Wenkiir")
⁠[分文不还](../Urchin.md#Give_Nothing_Back "Urchin")：从大谷仓拿走捐赠品。[孤儿](../Urchin.md "Urchin")背景。[玛德拉·死眼](../Maddala_Deadeye.md "Maddala Deadeye")

队伍必须从上锁的“捐赠箱”中拿取。
⁠[不道德的巧思](../Guild_Artisan.md#Immoral_Ingenuity "Guild Artisan")：发现陷阱玩具。[公会工匠](../Guild_Artisan.md "Guild Artisan")背景。[杰斯琳](../Jacelyn.md "Jacelyn")

队伍必须走到装有泰迪熊的箱子前，并通过被动调查检定。
