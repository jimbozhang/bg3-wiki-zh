## 野蛮人套装

主条目：[野蛮人套装](../Barbarian_set.md "Barbarian set")

**野蛮人套装**包含与[野蛮人](../Barbarian.md "Barbarian")机制互动的物品。[骨刺套装](../Bonespike_set.md "Bonespike set")和[怒火套装](../Wrath_set.md "Wrath set")是其一部分，但这些物品在此不重复列出。

- [理智之握](../Reason's_Grasp.md "Reason's Grasp")：选择[结束狂暴](../End_Rage.md "End Rage")时，获得15点[临时生命值](../Temporary_Hit_Points.md "Temporary Hit Points")。

## 吟游诗人（诗人激励）套装

主条目：[吟游诗人套装](../Bard_set.md "Bard set")

**吟游诗人套装**包含与[吟游诗人](../Bard.md "Bard")机制互动的物品。

- [慈善外套](../Blazer_of_Benevolence.md "Blazer of Benevolence")：当你使用[诗人激励](../Bardic_Inspiration.md "Bardic Inspiration")激励盟友时，你获得4点临时生命值。
- [响亮之靴](../Boots_of_Brilliance.md "Boots of Brilliance")：演奏你的乐器以恢复一个[诗人激励](../Bardic_Inspiration.md "Bardic Inspiration")使用次数（充能：长休）。
- [疗愈软帽](../Cap_of_Curing.md "Cap of Curing")：当你使用[诗人激励](../Bardic_Inspiration.md "Bardic Inspiration")激励盟友时，他们还会恢复1d6点生命值。
- [惊奇手套](../Wondrous_Gloves.md "Wondrous Gloves")：获得一次额外的[诗人激励](../Bardic_Inspiration.md "Bardic Inspiration")使用次数。

## 牧师套装

主条目：[牧师套装](../Cleric_set.md "Cleric set")

**牧师套装**包含与[牧师](../Cleric.md "Cleric")机制互动的物品、仅牧师可获得的物品，或施加[被驱散](../Turned_(Condition).md "Turned (Condition)")状态的物品。

- [虔诚护符](../Amulet_of_the_Devout.md "Amulet of the Devout")：获得一次额外的[引导神力充能](../Channel_Divinity_Charge.md "Channel Divinity Charge")和+2[法术豁免DC](../Spell_save_DC.md "Spell Save DC")。
- [狩猎饰环](../Circlet_of_Hunting.md "Circlet of Hunting")：对被[曳光弹](../Guiding_Bolt_(Condition).md "Guiding Bolt (Condition)")标记的生物进行[攻击掷骰](../Attack_roll.md "Attack Roll")时，获得**+1d4**加值。
- [信徒硬头锤](../Devotee's_Mace.md "Devotee's Mace")：可使用[治疗熏香灵光](../Healing_Incense_Aura.md "Healing Incense Aura")。仅通过[神圣干预](../Divine_Intervention.md "Divine Intervention")获得。
- [根德莱尔的渴望](../Gandrel's_Aspiration.md "Gandrel's Aspiration")：可使用[神圣弹药](../Sacred_Munitions.md "Sacred Munitions")。
- [圣星](../The_Sacred_Star.md "The Sacred Star")：击中时可[驱散](../Turned_(Condition).md "Turned (Condition)")不死生物。
- [清晨面纱](../Veil_of_the_Morning.md "Veil of the Morning")：可[驱散不死生物](../Turn_Undead.md "Turn Undead")。不死生物攻击时具有劣势。对抗不死生物造成的豁免检定时具有优势。

## 德鲁伊套装

主条目：[德鲁伊套装](../Druid_set.md "Druid set")

**德鲁伊套装**包含与[德鲁伊](../Druid.md "Druid")机制互动的物品，或增强变形和伪装的物品。

- [山崩护甲](../Armour_of_Landfall.md "Armour of Landfall")：可施放[植物滋长](../Plant_Growth.md "Plant Growth")。站在[植物滋长](../Plant_Growth_(Condition).md "Plant Growth (Condition)")或[藤蔓](../Difficult_Terrain_colon__Vines_(Condition).md "Difficult Terrain: Vines (Condition)")上时，恢复1d4点[生命值](../Healing.md "Healing")。
- [月照护甲](../Armour_of_Moonbasking.md "Armour of Moonbasking")：施放[荒野形态](../Wild_Shape.md "Wild Shape")后获得22点[临时生命值](../Temporary_Hit_Points.md "Temporary Hit Points")，并在这些临时生命值生效期间使受到的伤害减少1点。获得+2[护甲等级](../Armour_Class.md "Armour Class")和对抗法术的[豁免检定](../Saving_throw.md "Saving Throw")[优势](../Advantage.md "Advantage")；在[荒野形态](../Wild_Shape.md "Wild Shape")期间持续生效。
- [孢子守护者护甲](../Armour_of_the_Sporekeeper.md "Armour of the Sporekeeper")：可传播[噼啪砰孢子](../Bibberbang_Spores.md "Bibberbang Spores")、[鬼头蘑菇孢子](../Timmask_Spores_(class_action).md "Timmask Spores (class action)")和[加速孢子](../Haste_Spores.md "Haste Spores")。获得+1[法术豁免DC](../Spell_save_DC.md "Spell Save DC")，并在造成[黯蚀](../Necrotic.md "Necrotic")伤害时额外造成1点[黯蚀](../Necrotic.md "Necrotic")伤害。
- [硬木短棒](../Ironwood_Club.md "Ironwood Club")：当[橡棍术](../Shillelagh.md "Shillelagh")激活时，额外造成1d4点[钝击](../Bludgeoning.md "Bludgeoning")伤害。
- [铁藤盾牌](../Ironvine_Shield.md "Ironvine Shield")：当[橡棍术](../Shillelagh.md "Shillelagh")激活时，被近战攻击命中时造成[穿刺](../Piercing.md "Piercing")伤害。
- [残破甲壳](../Mutilated_Carapace.md "Mutilated Carapace")：变形或伪装时，攻击和伤害掷骰获得+1加值。
- [苍白橡树](../Pale_Oak.md "Pale Oak")：可施放[信仰守望者的藤蔓](../Faithwarden's_Vines.md "Faithwarden's Vines")。德鲁伊藤蔓无法[诱捕](../Ensnared_(Condition).md "Ensnared (Condition)")你，且不计入[劣势地形](../Difficult_Terrain_(Condition).md "Difficult Terrain (Condition)")。仅德鲁伊玩家角色可获得。
- [变形生物帽子](../Shapeshifter_Hat.md "Shapeshifter Hat")：获得一次额外的[荒野形态](../Wild_Shape.md "Wild Shape")使用次数。
- [变形者的恩赐之戒](../Shapeshifter's_Boon_Ring.md "Shapeshifter's Boon Ring")：变形时，所有[属性检定](../Ability_Check.md "Ability Check")获得+1d4加值。

## 战士套装

主条目：[战士套装](../Fighter_set.md "Fighter set")

**战士套装**包含与[战士](../Fighter.md "Fighter")机制互动的物品。

- [闪电誓缚战锤](../Charge-Bound_Warhammer.md "Charge-Bound Warhammer")：当被奥法骑士誓缚时，额外造成1d6点[闪电](../Lightning.md "Lightning")伤害。
- [玲珑手](../Gauntlets_of_Surging_Accuracy.md "Gauntlets of Surging Accuracy")：当你使用[动作如潮](../Action_Surge.md "Action Surge")时，本回合剩余时间内[攻击掷骰](../Attack_roll.md "Attack Roll")获得+1d4加值。
- [战神的铁手套](../Gauntlets_of_the_Warmaster.md "Gauntlets of the Warmaster")：目标对抗你的[策略](../Battle_Master.md#Level_3 "Battle Master")和[武器动作](../Weapon_actions.md "Weapon Actions")的[豁免检定](../Saving_throw.md "Saving Throw")具有[劣势](../Disadvantage.md "Disadvantage")。

## 武僧套装

主条目：[武僧套装](../Monk_set.md "Monk set")

**武僧套装**包含与[武僧](../Monk.md "Monk")机制互动的物品。另见[库席戈套装](../Kushigo_set.md "Kushigo set")、[灵魂套装](../Soul_set.md "Soul set")、[徒手套装](../Unarmed_set.md "Unarmed set")、[醉酒套装](../Drunk_set.md "Drunk set")，以及[与徒手打击互动的特性与物品列表](../List_of_features_and_items_that_interact_with_unarmed_strikes.md "List of features and items that interact with unarmed strikes")。

- [不羁库席戈之衣](../Armour_of_Uninhibited_Kushigo.md "Armour of Uninhibited Kushigo")：当[坚强防御](../Patient_Defence_(Condition).md "Patient Defence (Condition)")激活时，可使用反应对攻击未命中的攻击者进行徒手打击。
- [大地与天空之服](../Garb_of_the_Land_and_Sky.md "Garb of the Land and Sky")：使用[坚强防御](../Patient_Defence.md "Patient Defence")时获得[剑刃防护](../Blade_Ward_(Condition).md "Blade Ward (Condition)")。使用[飞檐走壁](../Step_of_the_Wind.md "Step of the Wind")后，下一次[攻击掷骰](../Attack_roll.md "Attack Roll")具有[优势](../Advantage.md "Advantage")，并额外造成1d8点[力场](../Force.md "Force")伤害。
- [感知护符（稀有）](../Sentient_Amulet_(Rare).md "Sentient Amulet (Rare)")：可使用[回气（次级）](../Ki_Restoration_(Lesser).md "Ki Restoration (Lesser)")。
- [感知护符（非常稀有）](../Sentient_Amulet_(Very_Rare).md "Sentient Amulet (Very Rare)")：可使用[回气（高级）](../Ki_Restoration_(Greater).md "Ki Restoration (Greater)")。
