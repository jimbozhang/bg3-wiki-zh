# 新鲜意志

The **Fresh Mind** is a unique item found in the [Mind Flayer Colony](Mind_Flayer_Colony.md "Mind Flayer Colony") in [Act Two](Act_Two.md "Act Two").

**新鲜意志**是在[第二幕](Act_Two.md "第二幕")的[夺心魔殖民地](Mind_Flayer_Colony.md "夺心魔殖民地")中发现的一种独特物品。

A humanoid brain - alive and in perfect condition - suspended in cerebrospinal fluid.

一个类人生物的大脑——存活且状态完美——悬浮在脑脊液中。

## 属性

## Where to find

## 获取地点

- In the [Mind Flayer Colony](Mind_Flayer_Colony.md "Mind Flayer Colony"), on-top of a stack of mucilaginous boxes beside the elevator leading to the final area

- 在[夺心魔殖民地](Mind_Flayer_Colony.md "夺心魔殖民地")中，位于通往最终区域的电梯旁一堆粘液盒子的顶部

## Usage

## 用途

- When placed on the Mind-Archive Interface within the [Mind Flayer Colony](Mind_Flayer_Colony.md "Mind Flayer Colony"), the Slack-Skinned Head speaks. Its dialogue implies that it is the mind of a child:
- My head hurts...
- Can I go back to bed, mummy?
- Reply: How did you get here?
- I live here. This is my house. _Our_ house. Right, mummy?
- Reply: I am very much _not_ your mother.
- Funny mummy!
- Reply: Well this is _deeply_ unsettling.
- I don't know what that means.
- I had that dream again. The big spiky snail that came from the sky.
- I'm _sleepy_ , mummy. You must stop waking me up.
- Reply: Uh, sure. Go right on back to sleep.
- Night night, mummy. Love you.
- _Any reply but "Well this is..."_
- We saw a big spiky snail that fell out of the sky!
- I'm just very sleepy, mummy... I wish you'd stop waking me up so much.
- Reply: Big spiky snail...?
- With big wiggly legs!
- Reply: Time to go back to sleep, then. Good night... _you_.
- Night night, mummy. Love you.
- Reply: I meanwhile am never going to sleep again.
- You should! We can talk in the morning.
- Night night, mummy. Love you.
- Reply: You'll never wake again, little one.
- W-what? You're scaring me!
- Why are you being so mean, mummy? Go away!
- When spoken to again:
- Sleepy... mummy...

- 当放置在[夺心魔殖民地](Mind_Flayer_Colony.md "夺心魔殖民地")内的大脑档案室接口时，皮肤松弛的脑袋会说话。其对话暗示它是一个孩子的心智：
- 我的头好痛...
- 我能回床上吗，妈妈？
- 回复：你是怎么到这里的？
- 我住在这里。这是我的房子。_我们_的房子。对吧，妈妈？
- 回复：我绝对_不是_你妈妈。
- 好笑的妈妈！
- 回复：嗯，这_非常_令人不安。
- 我不知道那是什么意思。
- 我又做了那个梦。那个从天上来的带刺大蜗牛。
- 我_困_了，妈妈。你必须停止叫醒我。
- 回复：呃，当然。继续睡吧。
- 晚安，妈妈。爱你。
- _任何回复，除了“嗯，这...”_
- 我们看到一只带刺大蜗牛从天上掉下来！
- 我只是很困，妈妈... 我希望你别再这么频繁地叫醒我了。
- 回复：带刺大蜗牛...？
- 还有会扭动的大腿！
- 回复：那么该回去睡觉了。晚安... _你_。
- 晚安，妈妈。爱你。
- 回复：而我则永远不会再睡觉了。
- 你应该睡！我们早上可以聊。
- 晚安，妈妈。爱你。
- 回复：你永远不会再醒来了，小家伙。
- 什-什么？你吓到我了！
- 你为什么这么坏，妈妈？走开！
- 当再次对话时：
- 困了... 妈妈...
