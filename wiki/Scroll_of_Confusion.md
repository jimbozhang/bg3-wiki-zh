# 困惑术卷轴

A **Scroll of Confusion** is a single-use scroll that allows the user to cast [Confusion](Confusion.md "Confusion") as an action.

## 属性

- [卷轴](Scrolls.md "Scrolls")

- 单次使用

- 稀有度：稀有

- 重量：0.02 kg (0.04 lb)

- 价格：180 gp

- UID `LOOT_SCROLL_Confusion` UUID `7634cc33-d16b-4640-ba7c-8c6a26653591` Stats `OBJ_Scroll_Confusion` ## 获取地点

- 由商人出售，在容器中找到，并由敌人携带
