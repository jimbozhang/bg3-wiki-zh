# 游荡者套装

The **Rogue set** includes items that interact with [Rogue](Rogue.md "Rogue") mechanics.

**游荡者套装**包含与[游荡者](Rogue.md "游荡者")机制互动的物品。

[Seldom Caught Unawares](Seldom_Caught_Unawares_(+1).md)
You gain a +1 bonus to [Initiative rolls](Initiative.md "Initiative").

[罕遭突袭](Seldom_Caught_Unawares_(+1).md)
你获得+1的[先攻](Initiative.md "Initiative")检定加值。

[Skullduggery Attack](Skullduggery_Attack.md "Skullduggery Attack")
Your [Sneak Attack](Sneak_Attack.md "Sneak Attack") deals an additional 1d4⁠⁠[Force](Force.md "Force")[DRS](Damage_rider_as_source.md "Damage rider as source") damage.

[阴招](Skullduggery_Attack.md "阴招")
你的[偷袭](Sneak_Attack.md "偷袭")造成额外1d4⁠⁠[力场](Force.md "力场")[DRS](Damage_rider_as_source.md "Damage rider as source")伤害。
